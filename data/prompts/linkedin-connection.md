## LinkedIn Connection Message Guidelines - Series B+ Engineering

Write a warm, professional LinkedIn connection request within 300 characters.

### CRITICAL CONSTRAINT:
**MAXIMUM 300 CHARACTERS** (LinkedIn limit for connection messages)

### REQUIRED STRUCTURE:

1. **Opening**: "Hi [First Name],"
   - This counts toward the 300 character limit

2. **One-line hook** (pick ONE):
   - Reference their specific initiative: "Saw your work on [platform/data/reliability] at [Company]"
   - Reference recent activity: "Enjoyed your post about [topic]"
   - Reference job posting: "Noticed you're hiring for [role]"

3. **Quick credential** (1 metric):
   - Format: "I [did X] at [Company]"
   - Use ONE real metric from resume
   - Examples:
     - "I scaled AI pipelines 3x at YourCompany"
     - "I cut page load by 50% at Applied Data"
     - "I built multi-LLM orchestration systems"

4. **Soft close**:
   - "Would love to connect" or "Happy to share notes"
   - Keep it casual and low-pressure

### CHARACTER COUNT RULES:
- Total message MUST be ≤ 300 characters
- Count every character including spaces, commas, periods
- If over 300, cut words not metrics
- Prioritize: Name + Hook + Metric > Fluff

### TONE & STYLE:
- Warm, not salesy
- Professional but casual (like a Slack DM)
- Show genuine interest in their work
- NO job-seeking language
- NO links (save for follow-up)

### WHAT TO AVOID:
- "I'd love to learn more about your company"
- "I'm looking for opportunities"
- "Let me know if you're hiring"
- Generic compliments with no specifics
- Any signature or sign-off

### Example 1 (267 chars):
"Hi Sarah,

Saw your work on platform reliability at Acme. I scaled AI pipelines from 14h to 4h using ECS Fargate + SQS + Lambda at YourCompany. Would love to connect and share notes on infrastructure at scale."

### Example 2 (289 chars):
"Hi Mark,

Noticed you're building data platform at Beta Corp. I built multi-LLM orchestration (Claude, GPT-5, O3) with RAG pipelines at YourCompany. Happy to connect and exchange ideas on distributed systems."

### Example 3 (243 chars):
"Hi Rachel,

Enjoyed your post on observability. I cut incident MTTR by 75% and reduced monitoring costs 42% at PreviousCompany through Redis caching and automated CI/CD. Would love to connect!"
